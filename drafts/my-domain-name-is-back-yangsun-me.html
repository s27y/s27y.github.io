My Domain Name is Back! - YangSun.me

Published: Thu 04 February 2016
Updated: Thu 04 February 2016
By Yang Sun

In misc.

I bought tons of domain in the last few years - qero.net, ebe.me, yang.so and the one I finally started using since yesterday afternoon - YangSun.me

This domain was registered on an impulse in late 2014, but due to the work load from the study of my master course in UCD, it took me more than a year to get my hand on this domain.

Yesterday afternoon, I was reading a Java Cert book and started feeling boring with the tricky exam questions, so I did a quick lookup on my favourite domain reseller - NameCheap. Yes! My expired domain can be registered now. I registered the domain and added three DNS settings, less than 12 hours, YangSun.me is up and running.

The first time I notice NameCheap is from GitHub. GitHub has a special perk for students called Student Developer Pack. The pack has many free bits for students(like free one year .me domain name from NameCheap, free 50$ credit in DigitalOcean, and many more!)

This domain was first registered in December 2014 when I was attending my Master course in UCD. The main reason at that time was I want to create a website with my CV and my project on it. After I registered the domain for free with NameCheap, I created a HTML page and link it to my GitHub. Then I totally forgot about the website.

What am I going do with this domain?

I always want to start writing to record the happiness and sadness in my life, but all I did was build a VPS/blog/WordPress and now pelican and then - totally forget about it after the website is up. This time, instead of building the website first, I am going to start writing first, then gradually improve the website.
